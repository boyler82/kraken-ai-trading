# BTC Intraday Mean Reversion v1

## Problem

D1 model misses intraday reversals.

## Hypothesis

When BTC makes an intraday oversold move and then reclaims short-term structure, a 1H/4H mean reversion trade may capture rebound to VWAP/MA20/resistance.

## Market

BTCUSD

## Timeframes

- 1H
- 4H

## Entry Ideas

- RSI(2) or RSI(6) oversold on 1H
- price deviation from 20-period moving average
- reclaim of previous 1H high
- no entry before confirmation

## Exit Ideas

- VWAP
- MA20
- previous local resistance
- fixed R multiple

## Invalidation

- new low after failed reclaim
- strong breakdown candle
- BTC below local support with expanding volume
