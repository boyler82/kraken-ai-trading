# Mean Reversion v1

## Goal

Buy weakness.
Sell rebound.

## Markets

- BTC
- ETH
- SPY
- QQQ
- NVDA
- GLD

## Entry Conditions

1. RSI(2) < 10
2. Price below MA20
3. No major breakdown
4. Reversal candle

## Exit

1. MA20 touch
2. Resistance
3. Risk Reward >= 1.5

## Invalidations

- Strong trend breakdown
- Macro event
- Volume expansion against position
